ProxyCleave ©: Restriction Digest Software

ProxyCleave is a restriction digest software capable of assessing multiple FASTA files to provide locations of restriction sites as would occur by use of one or more specificity. This specificity may include unspecified characters, and/or one or more degenerate nucleotides. Ive developed ProxyCleave over the last year.Its function is to provide a theoretical proof of recognition sites per given specififity within a given file or series of files.
