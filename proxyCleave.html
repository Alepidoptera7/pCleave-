ProxyCleave ©:

ProxyCleave is a restriction digest software capable of assessing multiple FASTA files to provide locations of restriction sites as would occur by use of one or more specificity.

This specificity may include unspecified characters, and/or one or more degenerate nucleotides.

It will be avaliable for non-commercial use.
